About Me

I started my career in law and worked in the litigation team of a boutique Sydney CBD law firm for a number of years, but I wanted to work in a ground-breaking industry and law just didn't do it for me. Now I am in my second year of Computer Science and am teaching first year courses at my uni as I go.

My main language is C which I teach and generally learn in, but I know Python too. I'm currently teaching myself a couple of scripting languages, as well as using this website as a lesson in HTML and CSS. Java is next on the list for later this year.

Away from the desk, you'd find me obsessing over the latest political intrigue or riding my motorbike out into the beautiful Hunter Valley, Southern Highlands or wherever else there's a nice road.

This website is a work in progress, and I'll be adding to it as I write blog posts and start projects. For now, check out my blog which I'll be updating over the May holidays with some smaller projects.
