    Tutor Feedback

    This feedback form is for my COMP1511 students. It can be used in two ways.

    First, it allows you to let me know if you are still confused about a topic or would like a refresher on anything we've covered so far. You can use this form whenever you like throughout semester, and I will do my best to either go over the topic again in tutorial (if we have time), or I will set aside some time in the lab to go over the content with whoever else wants to listen.

    Second, you can let me know if there's anything about my teaching I can improve on. If you let me know during semester, I can improve straight away rather than when everyone gets their feedback at the end of semester, so you get to benefit of your feedback!

    The form is completely anonymous (using https://usebasin.com). Only the text in the form below is sent to me, and only once you press 'Submit'.
